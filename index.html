Kaleidoscope is the official film festival of Engifest, conducted by Parchhayi, The photography and film making society of DTU. Works of art in the form of short films will be showcased and judged by prominent figures in the film industry.
Date: 14 Feb
Time: 12:00 pm
Venue: Edusat Hall
Team size: At most 15 (including the actors)
Time Limit: Max 15 minutes
Theme: No particular theme
Judging Criteria: Editing, Cinematography, Script, Acting, Sound design and Overall impact.
1. The movies should be either in Hindi or English or both.
2. If you choose to make a film in Hindi, it must contain English subtitles.
3. No other languages will be allowed.
4. Please note that we will not be responsible for postal delays, losses or damages–make sure you send in entries sufficiently before the deadline.
5. You may use any video camera you deem suitable for shooting the movie.
6. However, we strongly discourage low-resolution cameras, including webcams and mobile-phone cameras.
7. The content of the movie should be appropriate for public screening and thus should have no vulgarity.
8. Obscenity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.
9. Entries must be the original work of the entrants and must not infringe third-party's rights.
10. Please avoid photo slideshows, remixes, spoofs and advertisements.
11. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
12. Sound track can be borrowed, but with due credit.

Arte fotographia is the official Online photography competition of engifest conducted by parchhayi, the photography and film making society of DTU.
Themes
1. Night : Astro photography.
2. From where i stand : perspective photography.
3. Minimalism : abstract photography.
4. The four seasons : summer, spring , fall , winters.
5. Cityscapes : travel and architectural photography.

Entries to be sent to the email ID : parchhayidtu@gmail.com.
Date: Results on 13 Feb
Judging Criteria 1. 30% weightage to the relevance to the theme,caption,appeal and clarity.
2. The participants can send only one entry per theme, therefore a total of 5 photos maximum.
3. 10% to the mass appeal or the number of likes on Facebook.
Rules 1. There will be 5 themes.
2. We allow semiclassical styles provided there are distinctive classical elements are identified in the performance, amalgamated with other fusion styles. Contact the event coordinator in case of any queries.
3. All sorts of cameras are allowed. Excessive post-processing won't be allowed (no panoramas, no hdrs, no filters) please do note that the histogram of the entry sent to us will easily give away the use of any of the unaccepted methods.
4. EXIF to be mailed to us along with the photo. Photos should be in JPEG format with an appropriate caption.

Date: 14 Feb
Time: 12:00 am
Venue: Sports Complex
Number Of Rounds: 3
Team Size: 3 - 6 members (All college students). At least one Percussionist and two different instruments
Elimination: 1. Bands will be required to send in at least one recorded original compositions and one cover at arpeggiobotb@gmail.com, latest by 9th February 2016. The mail must include name of the band, name and college names of the band members and one contact number.
2. The covers can be improvised.
Top 10 entries will be selected for the semi-finals.
Semi-Finals 1. Bands will be required to perform at-least one original composition
2. The time limit has been set at 15 minutes (including set up, sound check and clearance time)
Top 5 bands will be selected for the finals.
Finals 1. Bands will be required to perform at-least one original composition and one cover
2. The theme of the final round is: “Ace of Spades: A tribute to Lemmy Kilmister”
3. The time limit has been set at 20 minutes (including set up, sound check and clearance time)

4. A standard 5-piece drum kit, keyboard stand, cables and Microphones will be provided.
5. Vocal Processors are not allowed; any band using vocal processors will be disqualified straightaway.
6. The decision of the judges would be final and irreversible.
