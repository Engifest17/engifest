Just a Minute

Get ready for this fast paced, 60 second linguistic thriller! This battle tests your quick thinking, speaking ability and command over the English language.
You got a minute to win it!

RULES
i. This is an individual event. However, more than one person from any college can participate.
ii. The participants will be divided into groups of 12 for the preliminary round.
iii. Top 12 scorers from the preliminary round will qualify for the final round.
iv. Participants will be judged on their clarity of thought, flow of speech, time for which they speak and on the refutations.
v. Refutations will be allowed on unnecessary repetitions of sentences, stammers, unnatural pauses, grammatical errors, mistimed starts, and other such errors.

POINTS TO REMEMBER
i. The JAM master is GOD
ii. Stick to point 1 to course smoothly through the competition.
iii. Any issues or discrepancies shall be resolved by the JAM master.
iv. The JAM master shall have the final verdict in all matters.
v. Keep in mind, that audibility and clarity of speech is key in this competition.
