Balladeers

Music is, certainly, the strongest form of magic- one that transcends all boundaries. So gather up your best wizards and bewitch the audience with your symphonies! Cast your spell at Balladeers!

REGISTRATION:
Participants can register both online and offline (on the spot).
Please note:
Only one person from a group should register.

RULES:
1. Each group is allotted a total of 12 minutes including sound check.
2. The number of members in one group can range between 5 and 25.
3. Use of instruments is not permitted at any time during the performance.
4. Beat boxing is allowed.
5. All song choices should be in English.
6. Only one group is allowed to participate from one college.

JUDGING CRITERIA:
Vocal dynamics, originality, creativity, coordination and overall impact.
